Our Story

Jack used to run every day in Stanley Park and I remember passing him once or twice. He caught my eye immediately but I was too shy to do anything. In the third time I saw him, it was Sunday and I had plans for brunch later on with my friends. He stopped me as we crossed paths and started talking to me. I guess he noticed me too :-) We planned to meet for brunch (sorry guys...) and from then on, it's all history. We've been inseparable ever since, also started jogging in the park together. After 8 months of dating, he proposed, in the exact same spot we first met, in Stanley Park. I was over the moon.

Getting to Bangalore

Flying: Try looking for flights into MakeMyTrip. Cabs or car services are available throughout the day and would cost around INR 600-1000.

Driving: Whether you’re flying in and renting or driving from elsewhere, having a car is good if you want to explore the area. If you are happy hanging at the venue, then don’t worry about a car.
